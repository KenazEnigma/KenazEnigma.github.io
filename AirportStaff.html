Airport Staff - Trainer Guide

Send two messages from the script at a time, keep it at a good pace and do not rush the scripts, as the trainees must be able to consume the information properly.

1. The commands to add/take away trainees points are listed as the following:

:addpoint user,user
:removepoint user,user

2. Once you have lined up with your trainees waiting to begin, you may state the following:


Section 0: Introduction






[Once the Host opens the barrier and announces the training may begin, the Lead Trainer may say the following:]



Section 1: Rules




































Section 2: Role Information












[The Assistant should show them what the check-in desk looks like. The trainees should remain seated.]



[The trainees should remain seated while the assistant shows them the gate area. Once done, the assistant should return to their spots.]



[Answer any necessary questions that pop up.]


Section 3: Greetings Portion












[The lead and assistant trainers should be checking and reading everyone's greeting. Add the trainees points as you check their greetings. Do not take too much time giving feedback.]


Section 4: Tickets & Boarding Portion








[Have the trainees line up nearby so they can easily watch the roleplay.]

[Do the short roleplay with your assistant. One being the passenger, the other being the Airport Staff. Ensure no trainees are being disruptive and that they are paying attention.]


[Demonstrate how to use the check-in desk AND the gate, be as detailed as possible like you would in a shift. Ensure it does not take longer than 5-7 minutes.]


Section 5: Quiz Portion










For the quiz questions, state one question per PM (total of 4) to the trainees using :PM. Allow each trainee to respond using the command to prevent cheating.


To keep track of who answered correctly, you may use :name username Correct.

Questions

What is one type of ticket passengers can recieve?
Answer: Economy, first class, business ticket



How do you hand out tickets?
Answer: Click the computer/at the front desk.



Do exploiters get warnings?
Answer: No warnings.



Which rank do you call for exploiters?
Answer: Any MR or HR.



How many warnings do trollers get?
Answer: One warning.




[Sort out who passed and who failed. Trainees who have passed should have an OVERALL score of 3/5. If anyone fails, you may dismiss and kick them.]









[The lead trainer will be responsible for ranking the trainees with the ranking tool.]
